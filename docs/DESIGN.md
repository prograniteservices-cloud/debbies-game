# 🌈 Unicorn Island Design System: Characters

This document serves as the **Source of Truth** for all visual assets in Debbie's Game. Use these specifications for all `fal-generate` and `threejs` assets.

## 🦄 Debbie (Protagonist)
- **Role:** Main character, guide, and player avatar.
- **Visual Description:** A vibrant, pastel purple unicorn with a shimmering rainbow mane and tail. Her horn glows with a soft golden aura.
- **Personality:** Optimistic, helpful, and playful.
- **Design Tokens:**
  - Primary Color: `#D8B4FE` (Soft Purple)
  - Secondary Color: `#FDF2F8` (Creamy White)
  - Accent: Rainbow Gradient (`#3B82F6`, `#10B981`, `#FBBF24`, `#EF4444`)
- **FAL Prompt Token:** `3D render, C4D, octane render, cute stylized unicorn, pastel purple, rainbow mane, sparkling eyes, unicorn island background, Pixar style, high detail, volumetric lighting`

## 🐲 Bubba (Antagonist/Brother)
- **Role:** Debbie's brother, often causes "hiccups" or challenges for the player.
- **Visual Description:** A slightly chubby, dark teal dragon-unicorn hybrid (dra-corn). He has a single stubby horn and small emerald wings.
- **Personality:** Mischievous but ultimately lovable; his "bad" actions are usually just accidents or curiosity.
- **Design Tokens:**
  - Primary Color: `#134E4A` (Dark Teal)
  - Secondary Color: `#BEF264` (Lime Accent)
  - Accent: Emerald Green Glow
- **FAL Prompt Token:** `3D render, C4D, octane render, cute mischievous dragon-unicorn hybrid, dark teal scales, stubby horn, tiny emerald wings, Pixar style, high detail, playful expression`

## 👦 Milo (New User Character - Male)
- **Role:** Selectable player character.
- **Visual Description:** A young boy (approx. 6 years old) wearing a starry explorer's vest and a magic headband. He has blue eyes and messy brown hair.
- **Personality:** Courageous, methodical, and observant.
- **Design Tokens:**
  - Primary Color: `#1E3A8A` (Midnight Blue)
  - Secondary Color: `#FACC15` (Star Yellow)
- **FAL Prompt Token:** `3D render, C4D, octane render, cute 6-year-old boy explorer, starry blue vest, magic headband, brown hair, blue eyes, Pixar style, joyful, high detail`

## 👧 Luna (New User Character - Female)
- **Role:** Selectable player character.
- **Visual Description:** A young girl (approx. 6 years old) with galaxy-themed braids and a sparkling cape. She wears moon-shaped earrings and a lavender tunic.
- **Personality:** Creative, intuitive, and calm.
- **Design Tokens:**
  - Primary Color: `#4C1D95` (Deep Lavender)
  - Secondary Color: `#E0E7FF` (Silver/Cloud)
- **FAL Prompt Token:** `3D render, C4D, octane render, cute 6-year-old girl, galaxy braids, sparkling cape, lavender tunic, silver accents, Pixar style, ethereal, high detail`

---

## 🎨 Global Aesthetic Guidelines
- **Rendering Style:** 3D Stylized (Pixar/Disney/Octane style).
- **Environment:** "Unicorn Island" - floating isles, candy-colored clouds, crystal waterfalls.
- **Sound Palette:** Whimsical, orchestral with synth sparkles.
